Beaver Mechanics + Real Facts

Wood Economy

Fact: Beavers fell trees with ever-growing incisors.

Mechanic: Forest lane workers produce more wood for dam upgrades.

Dam Construction

Fact: Real dams are built with branches, stones, and mud.

Mechanic: Spend wood + stone in the Dam Workshop for permanent upgrades.

Water Is Restorative

Fact: Beaver ponds create stable, protective wetland habitat.

Mechanic: River lanes and water income provide passive restoration.

Ecosystem Engineers

Fact: Beaver dams reshape landscapes and biodiversity.

Mechanic: Board space is split into River, Forest, and Dam habitats.
